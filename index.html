Welcome to the Neely Family Garden!

The garden was started in 2020 in the backyard of our house in Marana, AZ. It contains our favorite fruits, vegetables and flowers grown in raised garden beds and containers. We use natural products to repel pests and fertilize it with our kitchen compost. It is cared for by Sarah, Ben, and of course our dog Henry!
